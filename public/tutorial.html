First, enter the email address of the person you're sending to and press "Get Key"
With the key retrieved, type your message into the large text box and press "Encrypt."
Now, copy the encrypted text along with the instructions and send it as an email.
When you get an encrypted email, press the log in button on the decrypt page to authenticate with Google.
Once you're logged in, press get key to retrieve your private key, paste the decrypted part of the email into the box, and hit decrypt.
That's it! Now you can read the message knowing that it has remained secure since the time it was sent.
